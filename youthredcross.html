About Youth Red Cross

"Youth Red Cross" is a very important constituent of its mother organization, an extension of The Junior Red Cross. YRC Movement is based on three basic principles to develop the mental & moral capacities of the youth.

Our Fundamental Principles

Protection of Health & Life

Ensuring the well-being and safety of individuals through health awareness and life-saving skills

Service to the Sick & Suffering

Providing care and support to those in need through medical assistance and humanitarian aid

Promotion of Friendship

Fostering national and international understanding and cooperation among youth

Our Mission

To develop the mental and moral capacities of youth through humanitarian service and promote a culture of peace and understanding.

Youth Development

Empowering young people with leadership skills, humanitarian values, and community service experience.

Humanitarian Service

Engaging in activities that alleviate human suffering and promote dignity for all people.

Awards & Recognition

63rd Geneva Convention Day (2012-2013)

Mr A. Manoharan, lecturer, department of Mathematics and Youth Red Cross coordinator received the "Best Performer Award" from Vellore BDO for his outstanding contribution towards the Blood donation camp during the 63rd Geneva convention day.

The event was held on 13.08.12 by the Indian Red Cross Society, Vellore branch at the Vellore collectorate, recognizing exceptional service in humanitarian activities.
