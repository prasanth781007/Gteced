About ECE Department

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at GTEC was established in the year 2001 with an intake of 60 students. Over the years, the department has grown significantly and currently offers a 4-year B.E. program with an intake of 120 students.

Our department is committed to providing quality education and fostering research in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering. We have state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty members, and strong industry connections that provide our students with excellent learning opportunities.

The department focuses on emerging areas like VLSI Design, Embedded Systems, Wireless Communication, Internet of Things (IoT), and Signal Processing to prepare students for the rapidly evolving technological landscape.

Laboratories & Facilities

Electronics Circuits Laboratory

Hands-on facility for designing, building, and testing various electronic circuits including amplifiers, oscillators, and power supplies.

Key Equipment:
  • Digital Storage Oscilloscopes
  • Function Generators
  • Power Supplies
  • Breadboards & Components

Analog and Digital Devices Laboratory

Comprehensive lab for experimenting with both analog and digital electronic components and their applications.

Key Equipment:
  • Analog & Digital IC Testers
  • Logic Analyzers
  • Multimeters
  • Component Kits

DSP Laboratory

Facility for digital signal processing, image processing, and multimedia applications using advanced processors and software.

Key Equipment:
  • DSP Processors (TMS320 series)
  • MATLAB with Toolboxes
  • Image Processing Kits
  • Audio/Video Processing Equipment

VLSI Laboratory

State-of-the-art facility for VLSI design, FPGA programming, and integrated circuit testing and verification.

Key Equipment:
  • FPGA Development Boards
  • VLSI Design Software (Cadence, Xilinx)
  • IC Testing Equipment
  • Layout Tools

Communication System Laboratory

Equipped with advanced communication trainers, spectrum analyzers, and microwave test benches for comprehensive communication studies.

Key Equipment:
  • Digital Communication Trainers
  • Spectrum Analyzers
  • Antenna Training Systems
  • Modulation/Demodulation Kits

Optical and Microwave Laboratory

Specialized facility for experiments in optical fiber communication, microwave engineering, and RF systems.

Key Equipment:
  • Optical Fiber Communication Kits
  • Microwave Test Benches
  • Network Analyzers
  • Laser Diodes & Detectors

Microprocessor and Microcontroller Laboratory

Hands-on lab for programming and interfacing various microprocessors and microcontrollers for embedded applications.

Key Equipment:
  • 8085/8086 Microprocessor Kits
  • ARM/AVR/PIC Microcontroller Kits
  • Embedded Development Boards
  • Interface Modules

Networks Laboratory

Facility for studying computer networks, network protocols, and network security with practical implementation.

Key Equipment:
  • Network Simulators (Cisco Packet Tracer)
  • Router & Switch Configurations
  • Protocol Analyzers
  • Network Security Tools
