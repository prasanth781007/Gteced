Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs

  • Passed 10+2 examination with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved categories)
  • Valid score in JEE Main/TNEA or equivalent entrance exam
  • Age limit: Maximum 21 years for general category

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field with minimum 50% marks
  • Valid score in GATE/TANCET/CAT/MAT for respective programs
  • Work experience preferred for MBA program
  • No age limit for postgraduate programs

International Students

  • Equivalent qualification from recognized board/university
  • Valid student visa and passport
  • English proficiency test scores (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Medical fitness certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?

The application fee is ₹500 for general category and ₹250 for SC/ST candidates. The fee is non-refundable and can be paid online through credit card, debit card, or net banking.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, GTEC provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities. Hostel admission is based on availability and can be applied for along with the course application.

What is the admission criteria for NRI students?

NRI students must have equivalent qualifications recognized by the Indian education system. They need to submit valid student visa, passport copies, and proof of NRI status. Admission is based on merit and eligibility criteria.

Are there any management quotas?

Yes, a limited number of seats are available under management quota. Admission under this category is based on merit and personal interview. Fee structure for management quota seats is different from regular seats.

What is the refund policy?

If admission is cancelled before the commencement of classes, 90% of the tuition fee will be refunded. After commencement of classes, refund will be as per UGC guidelines. Application fee is non-refundable in all cases.
