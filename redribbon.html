About Red Ribbon Club

"Red Ribbon Club" formed in association with the Red Ribbon Clubs of USA and Tamilnadu strives for the welfare of the youth. The special features include teaching of Life Skill Techniques, and to create an awareness about HIV / AIDS. Job Orientation Programmes are also conducted through this club. This club has been specially implemented by the Tamil Nadu State Aids Control Society.

Our Mission

To empower youth with knowledge and life skills to prevent HIV/AIDS, reduce stigma and discrimination, and promote healthy living through awareness programs and community engagement.

Health Awareness

Creating comprehensive awareness about HIV/AIDS prevention, transmission, and treatment through educational programs and workshops.

Life Skills Training

Teaching essential life skills to help youth make informed decisions, resist peer pressure, and develop healthy relationships.

Career Guidance

Conducting job orientation programs and career counseling sessions to help students prepare for their professional lives.

Events & Activities

Celebrating Life (17.04.2014)

On behalf of our college RRC unit a programme titled "Celebrating Life" was conducted at EDUSAT Lab in our college on 17.4.14. Mr. ADISAYARUPAN, Resource person, TANSACS, Chennai, gave a special address about "AIDS awareness" to students.

More than 100 first year students participated the programme, gaining valuable knowledge about HIV/AIDS prevention and awareness.
