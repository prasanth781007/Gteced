About Rotaract Club

"ROTARACT CLUB" was organized by the department of Information Technology on August 14th 2014. The club brings together students to develop leadership skills, promote community service, and foster international understanding.

Our Mission

To provide opportunities for students to enhance knowledge and skills that will assist in personal development and address the physical and social needs of their communities.

Community Service

Engaging in various welfare activities to serve the needy in society and spread knowledge to make a positive impact.

Leadership Development

Building team coordination and organizational skills through various welfare activities and community engagements.

Installation Program 2014

Inaugural Ceremony - August 14, 2014

The Rotaract Club of GTEC was officially inaugurated on August 14th, 2014 with distinguished guests from the Rotary Club of Vellore. The installation program was graced by Rtn. PHF. S. RAJKUMAR (President), Rtn. PHF. PP. T. KRISHNAN (Director of Youth Service), and Rtn. PHF. M. BHIMA RAO (Secretary) of Rotary Club of Vellore.

Prof. D. DURA] KUMAR, Head of the Department of Information Technology and Coordinator of Rotaract Club, gave welcome address and Dr. R. VARATHARAJAN, Principal, honored the chief guests and gave his valuable address to the gathering.
