About Mechanical Engineering Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at GTEC was established to provide quality education in the field of mechanical sciences and engineering. Our department is committed to nurturing engineers who can design, analyze, and manufacture mechanical systems and components.

We focus on providing hands-on experience through well-equipped laboratories and workshops, preparing students for careers in various industries including automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, energy, and robotics.

The department emphasizes emerging areas like Robotics, Automation, Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing), Computational Fluid Dynamics, and Sustainable Energy Systems to prepare students for the evolving technological landscape.

Laboratories & Facilities

CAD/CAM Laboratory

Facility for computer-aided design and manufacturing with industry-standard software and equipment.

Key Equipment:
  • CAD Workstations
  • 3D Printers
  • CNC Machines
  • Design Software (AutoCAD, SolidWorks)

Thermal Engineering Laboratory

Comprehensive lab for studying thermodynamics, heat transfer, and energy conversion systems.

Key Equipment:
  • IC Engine Test Rigs
  • Heat Exchanger Setup
  • Refrigeration & AC Units
  • Boiler Models

Manufacturing Technology Laboratory

Hands-on facility for learning various manufacturing processes and techniques.

Key Equipment:
  • Lathe Machines
  • Milling Machines
  • Drilling Machines
  • Welding Equipment

Materials Science Laboratory

Facility for studying material properties, testing, and characterization.

Key Equipment:
  • Universal Testing Machine
  • Hardness Testers
  • Impact Testing Machine
  • Metallurgical Microscopes

Fluid Mechanics & Machinery Laboratory

Equipped with experimental setups for studying fluid properties and hydraulic machinery.

Key Equipment:
  • Flow Measurement Devices
  • Centrifugal Pump Test Rig
  • Pelton Wheel Turbine
  • Wind Tunnel

Robotics & Automation Laboratory

Specialized facility for robotics, automation, and control systems.

Key Equipment:
  • Industrial Robots
  • PLC Trainers
  • Automation Kits
  • Sensors & Actuators

Dynamics & Vibration Laboratory

Facility for studying mechanical vibrations, dynamics, and control systems.

Key Equipment:
  • Vibration Test Rigs
  • Balancing Machines
  • Whirling of Shaft Apparatus
  • Dynamic Analysis Software

Automobile Engineering Laboratory

Facility for studying automotive systems, components, and performance analysis.

Key Equipment:
  • Engine Test Benches
  • Chassis Dynamometer
  • Automotive Components
  • Diagnostic Equipment
